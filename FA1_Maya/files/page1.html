Girl From Nowhere

  • The show is about a girl named Nanno whose job is to bring justice to those who deserve it. She goes from school to school punishing all the lies and wrongdoings the people around her make.
  • Every episode talks about different social issues and although sometimes the happenings stray away from reality, it is still an exciting way to learn lessons.
  • As of September 2022, Girl from nowhere had 2 seasons, and we expect the 3rd season.
