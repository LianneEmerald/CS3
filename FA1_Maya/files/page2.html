Squid Game

  • The show is about a divorced father, Seong Gi-hun, who is drowning in debt and takes the mysterious offer of a man to play games to win money. He and the other 445 other players later learn that they are to play children's games but with a twist.
  • The first episode introduces our main character and all of his life's troubles. Then, the other episodes show him and the other players fighting, making and breaking alliances, deceiving, and lying to gain the cash prize.
  • As of September 2022, Squid Game had 1 season, and we expect the 2nd season.
